Lucky

8/12/2018

This is a fun tribute page to our loyal companions!

He loves to go for a walk and to play fetch. He will also hide under the coaches.

  1. His favorite treats are bones
  2. He loves to lay on blankets, either on the coach or the floor
  3. He gets real excited around dinner time

Q&A

Questions

  1. What is his favorite toy?
  2. Who is his favorite human?
  3. What does he do when he needs someone's attenion?

Answers

  1. Just plain tennis balls. He will play fetch with those till the end of time
  2. I would like to say me, but it would probably be the one who feeds him the most (my mom)
  3. He will just sit there and stare at them. When they finally get the memo he will get up and lead them to what he needs (e.g to the back door to go outside, or to his water bowl to refill it).
